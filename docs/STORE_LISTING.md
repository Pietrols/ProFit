## Full description

ProFit is a workout and nutrition companion built around one honest idea:
what you *actually* did matters more than what you planned.

**Plan it** — pick bodybuilding, powerlifting, CrossFit, cardio, or mix them
into a custom week. Choose home or gym and every exercise fits your
equipment, with curated home alternatives.

**Do it** — a focused active-workout screen with session and rest timers,
quick rep/weight entry, exercise swaps that respect your equipment, and
warm-up/cool-down checklists.

**Log what really happened** — skipped sets, changed weights, cut-short
sessions. ProFit records the difference between plan and reality instead of
pretending it didn't happen.

**Let it adapt** — your next session adjusts from that honesty: progress
when you complete, back off when you struggle, and a real deload when a
week of check-ins says you're wrecked. Optional AI coaching sharpens
adjustments, suggests meal swaps that fit food you already eat, and answers
questions in a chat that knows your plan and progress.

**Everything works offline.** Workouts, meals, and check-ins are saved on
your phone first and sync when you're back online — no signal in the gym
basement, no problem.

Also in the box: exercise library with demo images and equipment filters,
strength curves and weekly volume per muscle group, adherence and streaks,
bodyweight trend, meal logging with zero calorie-counting guilt, training-day
reminders, and one-tap export of *your* data (JSON/CSV).

No ads. No analytics. Your logs are yours.

ProFit is not medical advice — consult a professional before starting a new
program.
